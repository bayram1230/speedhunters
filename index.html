TAKE A LOOK AT OUR MOST WANTED

Dodge Charger

A classic Dodge Charger, particularly from the late 1960s and early 1970s, is a muscle car known for its aggressive styling, powerful V8 engines, and prominent role in American automotive history and pop culture. These models are highly sought after by collectors and enthusiasts.

Chevrolet Chevelle SS

The Chevrolet Chevelle SS is a classic American muscle car, known for its powerful engines, aggressive styling, and strong performance. Available from 1964 to 1972, the SS (Super Sport) trim offered upgraded engines like the 396 and 454 V8s.

Shelby Cobra

The Shelby Cobra, also known as the AC Cobra in Europe, is a high-performance sports car created by Carroll Shelby. Combining British design and an American Ford V8 engine, it is iconic for its raw power and distinctive look.
